Service

STEMfest

I was a part of a 10-person team that planned and organized a Girls Scout event called STEMfest. This event was aimed for girls in elementary to middle school and showcased different engineering organizations performing quick experiments.

SWEOutreach Committee

SWEOutreach aims to raise awareness about other discplines within engineering and break stigmas about STEM majors to K-12 students. On this committee, I helped plan and demonstrate small engineering projects at a local elementary school twice a month. In addition, a team of 10 people and I planned for another event similar to STEMFest but for high school students with harder STEM related challenges.

More

Along with being in SWE, freshman year I was a part of NSBE's outreach program, Technical Outreach Community Help (T.O.R.C.H). This program was similar to SWEOutreach, but went to a local Boys & Girls club instead of local elementary schools. In addition to the outreach I've done, I would like to get more involved with my community by joining a service club and volunteering with them. In highschool, I volunteered monthly with one of San Antonio's homeless shelters, Haven for Hope. While I was there I would serve and prep food or stock and sort Food bank items in the building's kitchen. In college station, my college town, I would like to volunteer at an animal shelter or food bank in order to give back to the community I've been a part of for the past couple of years.
